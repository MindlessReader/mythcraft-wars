====================================
  SKILL PROGRESSION
====================================
All classes share the same skill tree.
Enchantments are applied automatically based on your team's skill levels.


------------------------------------
  ATTACK (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1-5:
    Iron Sword    -> Sharpness I-V
    Iron Axe      -> Sharpness I-V
    Iron Spear    -> Sharpness I-V
    Bow           -> Power I-V
    Crossbow      -> Quick Charge I-V
    Mace          -> Density I-V
  Level 3+:
    Iron Sword    -> Sweeping Edge III


------------------------------------
  DEFENSE (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Shield granted (if you don't already have one)
    All Armor     -> Protection I-V (scales with level)
  Level 3+:
    All Armor     -> Thorns III


------------------------------------
  MAGIC (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Seeking Breath spell unlocked
  Level 3+:
    Iron Sword    -> Knockback II
    Iron Spear    -> Lunge III

  Seeking Breath Cooldowns:
    Level 1: Cold      (30s)
    Level 2: Lukewarm  (15s)
    Level 3: Warm      (10s)
    Level 4: Hot       ( 5s)
    Level 5: Boiling   ( 3s)


------------------------------------
  SPECIAL (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Boots         -> Frost Walker II
  Level 2+:
    Leggings      -> Swift Sneak III
  Level 3+:
    Bow           -> Infinity
    Crossbow      -> Multishot
  Level 4+:
    Bow           -> Flame
    Crossbow      -> Piercing IV
  Level 5:
    Iron Sword    -> Fire Aspect II
    Mace          -> Wind Burst III


====================================
  NOTES
====================================
- Each weapon only appears for the classes that use it.
  Enchantments only apply if you have that weapon in your kit.
- City bonuses and quest rewards stack on top of class gear.
- Your class persists across deaths.
