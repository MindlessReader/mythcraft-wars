====================================
  PROGRESSION SYSTEM
====================================
Mythcraft Wars has TWO progression systems that work together:

1. CHARACTER LEVELS (Per-Player, Levels 1-5)
   - Earned through combat XP
   - XP Sources: +1 per troop kill, +3 per player kill
   - Affects: Gear material tier (Iron/Diamond/Netherite),
             armor & toughness attributes, and some enchantments

2. TEAM SKILLS (Shared by Team, Levels 0-5)
   - Four skill locations: Attack, Defense, Magic, Special
   - Earned by your team capturing and holding skill locations
   - Affects: Weapon & armor enchantments for all team members


====================================
  CHARACTER LEVEL ENCHANTMENTS
====================================
These enchantments scale with YOUR personal character level:

------------------------------------
  SPEAR (Warrior Only)
------------------------------------
  Levels 1-2: Lunge I
  Levels 3-4: Lunge II
  Level 5:    Lunge III

------------------------------------
  CROSSBOW (Warrior & Assassin)
------------------------------------
  Character level directly scales Quick Charge:
    Level 1: Quick Charge I
    Level 2: Quick Charge II
    Level 3: Quick Charge III
    Level 4: Quick Charge IV
    Level 5: Quick Charge V

------------------------------------
  MACE (Bastion Only)
------------------------------------
  Levels 2-3: Density I
  Level 4:    Density II
  Level 5:    Density III


====================================
  TEAM SKILL ENCHANTMENTS
====================================
These enchantments are granted to ALL team members based on
your team's skill levels at the four skill locations.


------------------------------------
  ATTACK (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1-5:
    Sword         -> Sharpness I-V
    Axe           -> Sharpness I-V
    Spear         -> Sharpness I-V
    Bow           -> Power I-V
  Level 3+:
    Sword         -> Sweeping Edge III


------------------------------------
  DEFENSE (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Shield granted (if you don't already have one)
    All Armor     -> Protection I-V (scales with level)
  Level 3+:
    All Armor     -> Thorns III


------------------------------------
  MAGIC (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Seeking Breath spell unlocked
  Level 3+:
    Sword         -> Knockback II

  Seeking Breath Cooldowns:
    Level 1: Cold      (30s)
    Level 2: Lukewarm  (15s)
    Level 3: Warm      (10s)
    Level 4: Hot       ( 5s)
    Level 5: Boiling   ( 3s)


------------------------------------
  SPECIAL (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Boots         -> Frost Walker II
  Level 2+:
    Leggings      -> Swift Sneak III
  Level 3+:
    Bow           -> Infinity
    Crossbow      -> Multishot
  Level 4+:
    Bow           -> Flame
    Crossbow      -> Piercing IV
  Level 5:
    Sword         -> Fire Aspect II
    Mace          -> Wind Burst III


====================================
  NOTES
====================================
- Each weapon only appears for the classes that use it.
  Enchantments only apply if you have that weapon in your kit.
- Character level affects YOUR gear quality and personal stats.
- Team skills affect enchantments for your ENTIRE team.
- City bonuses and quest rewards stack on top of everything.
- Your class and character level persist across deaths.
- Gear automatically upgrades when you reach character levels 3 and 5.
