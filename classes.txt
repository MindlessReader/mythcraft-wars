====================================
  MYTHCRAFT WARS - CLASS REFERENCE
====================================

Switch class anytime: /trigger selectClass set <1/2/3>
(Instantly re-equips you with your new class gear)

    Level 1: 10 armor, 0 toughness

    Level 3: 14 armor, 2 toughness (Iron → Diamond)
    Level 4: 16 armor, 3 toughness

------------------------------------
  ASSASSIN
  /trigger selectClass set 2
------------------------------------
  Slot 1: Sword (material scales with character level)
  Slot 2: Bow
  Slot 3: Crossbow
  Slot 8: Compass
  Slot 9: Steak x64
  Offhand: Chargeable Totem of Undying
  Armor:  Full Leather (material scales with character level)

  Chargeable Totem:
    - Starts at 0 charge (durability bar shows progress)
    - Charge by killing: +1 per troop kill, +3 per player kill
    - Max charge decreases with character level:
        Level 1: 64  |  Level 2: 48  |  Level 3: 36
        Level 4: 24  |  Level 5: 16
    - Fully charged: grants death protection (saves from lethal damage)
      Death save effects: Regen 1 (10s), Absorption 2 (5s), Speed 2 (30s),
      Invisibility (15s), Resistance 2 (10s). Charge resets to 0.
    - At 25%+ charge: right-click with sword to consume 25% charge
      Activation effects: Speed 2 (30s) to self + allies within 10 blocks,
      Invisibility (15s) to self only, armor + totem hidden for 15s
    - Charge persists through death, resets on class change
    - Requires Mythcraft resource pack for invisible armor/totem models

  Character Level Attributes:
    Level 1: 6 armor, 0 toughness
    Level 2: 7 armor, 0 toughness
    Level 3: 9 armor, 1 toughness (Leather → Diamond)
    Level 4: 11 armor, 2 toughness
    Level 5: 13 armor, 3 toughness (Diamond → Netherite)


------------------------------------
  BASTION
  /trigger selectClass set 3
------------------------------------
  Slot 1: Axe (material scales with character level)
  Slot 2: Crossbow
  Slot 3: Mace
  Slot 8: Compass
  Slot 9: Steak x64
  Offhand: Shield
  Armor:  Full Iron (material scales with character level)

  Perfect Parry:
    - Raise shield then block within 0.5s for a Perfect Parry
    - Blocking outside the window triggers a lesser Normal Parry
    - Perfect: damage + Slowness II (3s) on attacker, Resistance I (1s) self

        Perfect:  Level 1-2: 3 hearts | Level 3-4: 4 hearts | Level 5: 5 hearts

    - Only reflects to enemies within 5 blocks (melee range)
    - Bastion-only (other classes with Defense shields cannot parry)

  Character Level Attributes:
    Level 1: 14 armor, 1 toughness
    Level 2: 16 armor, 2 toughness
    Level 3: 17 armor, 4 toughness (Iron → Diamond)
    Level 4: 18 armor, 6 toughness
    Level 5: 20 armor, 8 toughness (Diamond → Netherite)


====================================
  PROGRESSION SYSTEM
====================================
Mythcraft Wars has TWO progression systems that work together:

1. CHARACTER LEVELS (Per-Player, Levels 1-5)
   - Earned through combat XP
   - XP Sources: +1 per troop kill, +3 per player kill
   - Affects: Gear material tier (Iron/Diamond/Netherite),
             armor & toughness attributes, and some enchantments

2. TEAM SKILLS (Shared by Team, Levels 0-5)
   - Four skill locations: Attack, Defense, Magic, Special
   - Earned by your team capturing and holding skill locations
   - Affects: Weapon & armor enchantments for all team members


====================================

------------------------------------

------------------------------------
  Levels 2-3: Density I
  Level 4:    Density II
  Level 5:    Density III


====================================
  TEAM SKILL ENCHANTMENTS
====================================
These enchantments are granted to ALL team members based on
your team's skill levels at the four skill locations.


------------------------------------
  ATTACK (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1-5:
    Sword         -> Sharpness I-V
    Axe           -> Sharpness I-V
    Spear         -> Sharpness I-V
    Bow           -> Power I-V
  Level 3+:
    Sword         -> Sweeping Edge III


------------------------------------
  DEFENSE (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Shield granted (if you don't already have one)
    All Armor     -> Protection I-V (scales with level)
  Level 3+:
    All Armor     -> Thorns III


------------------------------------
  MAGIC (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Seeking Breath spell unlocked
  Level 3+:
    Sword         -> Knockback II

  Seeking Breath Cooldowns:
    Level 1: Cold      (30s)
    Level 2: Lukewarm  (15s)
    Level 3: Warm      (10s)
    Level 4: Hot       ( 5s)
    Level 5: Boiling   ( 3s)


------------------------------------
  SPECIAL (Levels 1-5)
------------------------------------
  Level 1+:
    Boots         -> Frost Walker II
  Level 2+:
    Leggings      -> Swift Sneak III
  Level 3+:
    Bow           -> Infinity
    Crossbow      -> Multishot
  Level 4+:
    Bow           -> Flame
    Crossbow      -> Piercing IV
  Level 5:
    Sword         -> Fire Aspect II
    Mace          -> Wind Burst III


====================================
  NOTES
====================================
- Each weapon only appears for the classes that use it.
  Enchantments only apply if you have that weapon in your kit.
- Character level affects YOUR gear quality and personal stats.
- Team skills affect enchantments for your ENTIRE team.
- City bonuses and quest rewards stack on top of everything.
- Your class and character level persist across deaths.
- Gear automatically upgrades when you reach character levels 3 and 5.
